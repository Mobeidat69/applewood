Moe Mahasneh – Product Advisor

Welcome to Applewood Nissan/Infiniti in Richmond, Vancouver! My name is Moe Mahasneh, and I am thrilled to be your dedicated Product Advisor. With years of experience in the automotive industry, I specialize in guiding customers through the exciting journey of finding their perfect vehicle. Whether you're interested in the latest Nissan and Infiniti models or exploring our extensive selection of high-quality used cars from various makes, I am here to provide you with personalized and expert assistance.

At Applewood Nissan/Infiniti, we believe that buying a car should be a joyful and stress-free experience. That's why I prioritize understanding your unique needs and preferences, ensuring you make an informed decision that you’ll be delighted with for years to come. Whether we connect in the dealership, online, or over the phone, I am committed to offering top-notch service and making every interaction count.

I invite you to set up an appointment with me today. Together, we’ll navigate the features, benefits, and options available to you, and I’ll make sure you drive away with a smile. Let’s turn your car buying dreams into reality!
